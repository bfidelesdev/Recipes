Chocolate Cake

Ingredientes

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of powdered chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 cups of flour (wheat)
  • 2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of yeast
  • 1 cup (tea) milk

Modo de fazer

  1. In a blender add the eggs, chocolate powder, butter, wheat flour, sugar and milk, then beat for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the yeast and mix with a spatula gently.
  3. In a greased shape, pour the dough and bake in a medium oven (180ยบ C) preheated for about 40 minutes. Don't forget to use a tall mold for this recipe: as it takes two spoons of yeast, it rises a lot! Another solution can be to put just a spoonful of yeast and keep your recipe in a small form.
